With the release of Star Wars: Rogue One, many fans were dissapointed that the new movie was sans of the franchises famous "crawl".

But as this is a prequel to the original series, it was decided that if the makers were to include the crawl it would be too extensive to add to the opening scene. One must remember that this is the prequel to Episode IV A New Hope.

Instead they opted for a prologue to the film that is set back 15 years before our story begins...
